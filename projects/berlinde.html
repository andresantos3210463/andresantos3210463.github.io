Briefing

Digital prototyping

In this brief we had to create a postcard about a traditional game, and convey this message simply and with a color palette summarized in black and white and one other color of our choice. This is how a poster was created referring to the traditional game “marbles”, with the elements of marbles and the hole where they have to hit them. In terms of color, we selected the color yellow to relate to the environment where it was played, on the sand. In order to create an appealing, informative and nostalgic poster. As this work was carried out to be placed in a school context, the name of the poster was placed in the local language, in this case Portuguese.
