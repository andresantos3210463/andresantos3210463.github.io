Cover for the Magazine of the Year
Externato de penafirme

In this briefing, the objective was to create a cover for the magazine of the year at the Externato de Penafirme school. The cover would have to identify the school, the year, the director, the person responsible for general coordination, whoever created the cover and finally the printer that was responsible for printing the magazines. This cover was a schoolwide winner and 550 copies were printed.

This project was done in the second year of my first Multimedia course.
