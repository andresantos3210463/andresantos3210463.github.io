Project Spotify Canvas
School subject digital prototyping

In this brief we had to create a short video for Spotify canvas in order to create something artistic related to a song of our liking. So I chose the song Gangsta's Paradise by Coolio, and I started thinking about what to do to relate to the theme of the song, which gave rise to the idea of making a 3D clock passing the hours quickly in order to relate to the chorus of the song. song, "Been spendin' most their lives Livin' in a gangsta's paradise". Furthermore, I wanted to give an antique effect, so, in post-production, I added the old tape effect.

Keywords:
Gangsters, Crime, Equality, Freedom, Death
