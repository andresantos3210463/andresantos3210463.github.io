Briefing

My passion for the 3D area began when I accepted the first challenge proposed by the company Gate 7 (My first internship), a reference in the animation and visual effects market. It was thanks to this opportunity that I was able to develop my skills and advance in my professional career. The objective of this work was to make a 3D doll that held a bottle of wine from Manzwine, a wine company, to promote its brand.

This project was done in the second year of my first Multimedia course on internship with Gate7.
